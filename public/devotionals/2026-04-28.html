I Won't Repent

Pastor David Ofurum

1Samuel 15:29 KJV And also the Strength of Israel will not lie nor repent: for he is not a man, that he should repent.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: My word remains true for all time because I have neither the capacity to lie nor the tendency to renege. I have uttered My good plans for you, and that will never change. What I have said about your glorious future stands sure. I have all it takes to fulfill whatever I say.

TO DO: DECLARE: Father, I thank You for the certainty of Your word. You have a track record of keeping Your word. If You have said it, then You will do it. That’s why I call You Faithful, Dependable, and True. I can count on You. So, I build my life on the solid rock of Your word that never fails.

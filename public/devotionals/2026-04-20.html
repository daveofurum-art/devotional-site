It's Not Over

Pastor David Ofurum

Psalms 16:10 KJV For thou wilt not leave my soul in hell; neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: You may be going through hell right now, and it might seem like it will always be like this. But it’s not the end of the road for you. I have promised that I will not let your story end in destruction. Believe Me when I say it’s only a phase, and I’m bringing you out of it. Hang on.

TO DO: CONFESS: It’s not over for me. I’m not finished. It’s not the end. This bad episode is not where my story ends. I’m more than a conqueror through Jesus Who loves me. I will not see corruption, but this shall pass. This is only my praise being processed; it will end in praise.

I Am Your Backing

Pastor David Ofurum

Romans 8:31 KJV What shall we then say to these things? If God be for us, who can be against us?

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Who is he that can harm you when I am standing solidly with you, with all My power at your disposal? The Greater One lives in you; why should you fear the lesser one who is in the world? Remember, your life is hidden with Christ in God. Anyone seeking your hurt must deal with Me first.

TO DO: CONFESS: In Christ, I live, move, and have my being. Everything about me is under Heaven’s unbreakable protective surveillance. In all things, I walk with confidence, knowing that I have the strongest backing. Any attack against me is a total failure. Glory be to God!

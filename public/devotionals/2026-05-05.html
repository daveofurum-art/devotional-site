Pay Your Breath Rent

Pastor David Ofurum

Psalm 150:6 KJV Let every thing that hath breath Praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: When you rent a property, you pay rent as the basis for your continued occupancy of that property. If you breathe in Oxygen, your praise is the rent you pay Me for it, and this rent is due every second. What are the implications of a rent default? Have you paid your rent today?

TO DO: DECLARE: I woke up this morning; I won’t be quiet. I have breath in my lungs, life in my body, and joy in my spirit; I will praise the Lord. Irrespective of my circumstances, I will not default on my rent. As long as I’m breathing, I have a reason to praise the Lord. Hallelujah!

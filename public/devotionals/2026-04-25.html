I Am Power

Pastor David Ofurum

Psalms 62:11 KJV God hath spoken once; twice have I heard this; that power belongeth unto God.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: You have not experienced power until you have experienced Me. I am the very Source of power. All the strength and ability you need for anything you need to be or achieve comes from Me; you need not look elsewhere. Now, receive power. Become all you need to be.

TO DO: DECLARE: I receive it, Lord! I receive the power to become everything You’ve destined for me. My days of struggling are over. I now proceed in Your mighty power. I conquer my world by Your power, and I return daily with great testimonies in Jesus’ name. Amen.

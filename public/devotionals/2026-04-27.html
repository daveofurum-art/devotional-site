Where's Your Mind?

Pastor David Ofurum

Isaiah 26:3 KJV Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Your perfect peace is not guaranteed if your mind keeps drifting from Me. Stop wandering from present problems to future concerns, to things over which you have no control. In order to enjoy perfect peace, you need to keep your mind fixed on Me. Always ask yourself, “Where's my mind right now?"

TO DO: DECREE: My mind will not lose focus on God. The issues of life will not distract me from looking unto Jesus, the Author and Finisher of my faith. God is the only reality; my challenges are nothing but noisy distractions. Therefore, my confidence will never shift from God.

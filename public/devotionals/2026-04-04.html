Saturday, April 04, 2026 - HAPPY HOLY SATURDAY

Despite Your Mess

Pastor David Ofurum

Matthew 12:40 KJV For as Jonas was three days and three nights in the whale's belly; so shall the Son of man be three days and three nights in the heart of the earth.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Jonah's disobedience to Me landed him in the whale's belly. How then could it become a sign for the Messiah’s death and grave travails? Well, that tells you that no matter how badly you’ve messed up, I can still make a message out of your mess if you yield to My will today.

TO DO: PRAY: Dear Father, I surrender my life, my will, my plans, my opinions, and my desires to You this day. I may be broken and messy, but if You could make something positive out of Jonah’s mess, then You can make me what You want me to be. Take over, Lord, in Jesus’ name. Amen.

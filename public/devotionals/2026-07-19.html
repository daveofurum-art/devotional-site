Are You Living?

Pastor David Ofurum

John 5:24 KJV Verily, verily, I say unto you, He that heareth my word, and believeth on him that sent me, hath everlasting life, and shall not come into condemnation; but is passed from death unto life.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: All humans are born spiritually dead because of sin, but life is restored when they put their faith in Me. Many are physically living, but without Me, they are spiritually dead and headed for eternal condemnation; they are living dead. The question today is: Are you fully living or living dead?

TO DO: DECLARE: Jesus Christ, the Son of God, I believe in You and the Father Who sent You. I believe that salvation, redemption, justification, and eternal life are found only in You. I accept You as My personal Lord and Saviour. I receive Your life, and I’m Yours forever. I have passed from death to life. Hallelujah!

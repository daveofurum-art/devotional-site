Protocol of Blessing

Pastor David Ofurum

Numbers 6:23 KJV Speak unto Aaron and unto his sons, saying, On this wise ye shall bless the children of Israel, saying unto them,

THUS SAYS THE LORD: There is a procedure for blessing, which I ordained from the foundation of the world; it is called “speaking”. When I blessed man at creation, I used this same protocol. So, be mindful of the words you speak. If you want to bless anything, just speak to it.

TO DO: DECLARE: I speak to my life, my health, my family, and the works of my hands: It is well with you. God’s favour is upon you. All things align for your good. You excel in all things. The blessing of God, which makes rich and adds no sorrow, is evident in all that concerns you in Jesus’ name. Amen.

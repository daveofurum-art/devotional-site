Friday, April 03, 2026 - HAPPY GOOD FRIDAY

Why The Celebration?

Pastor David Ofurum

John 3:16 KJV For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Remember Isaiah 9:6? Unto us a child is born – that’s “Christmas”, the manger. Unto us a son is given – that’s “Good Friday”, the cross. I gave My only Son to die so you can live the life of God – that’s good news. The manger and the cross are both something to celebrate.

TO DO: DECLARE: At the cross, where I first saw the light, and the burden of my heart rolled away. It was there by faith, I received my sight, and now I am happy all the day. Thank You for the cross, Lord, for the price You paid; bearing all my sin and shame. In love, You came and gave amazing grace.

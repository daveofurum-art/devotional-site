I See His Moves

Pastor David Ofurum

Job 1:7 KJV And the LORD said unto Satan, Whence comest thou? Then Satan answered the LORD, and said, From going to and fro in the earth, and from walking up and down in it.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Did you really think I didn’t know what Satan had been up to? I am the all-seeing and all-knowing; nothing is hidden from Me. Before Satan plans it, I have seen it. Sometimes, I nip it in the bud, and sometimes, I just let him exhaust his foolery before I show him who’s the boss. So, stay calm.

TO DO: PRAY: When Satan rages wild like a roaring lion around me, Lord, give me the grace to stay calm, knowing that You see it all and You have everything under control. Let my knowledge of Your Almightiness still my heart in trust and peace until Your hand is fully revealed in Jesus’ name. Amen.

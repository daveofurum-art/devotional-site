Sprinkle The Blood

Pastor David Ofurum

Exodus 24:8 KJV And Moses took the blood, and sprinkled it on the people, and said, Behold the blood of the covenant, which the LORD hath made with you concerning all these words.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: The blood of the covenant is potent by contact. For everyone and everything under your care, for whom you want the blood of the covenant to avail, sprinkle the blood upon them. In the new covenant, you sprinkle the blood of Jesus by speaking it in faith over that upon which you want it to have effect.

TO DO: DECLARE: I sprinkle the blood of Jesus over my family, my home, my assets, my paths, and the works of my hand [add other people and things you wish to]. I bring them under the influence of the blood of Jesus. It shall only be with them as is consistent with the covenant of better things in the blood of Jesus Christ. Amen.

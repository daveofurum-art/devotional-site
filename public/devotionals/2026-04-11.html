I Feel You

Pastor David Ofurum

Hebrews 4:15 KJV For we have not an high priest which cannot be touched with the feeling of our infirmities; but was in all points tempted like as we are, yet without sin.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: I came to earth in human form to know what it feels like to be you. The pain in your body, the hurt in your heart, the feeling of frustration, disappointment, helplessness, and all, I felt them. I can relate, and I know just what to do to unburden you. So, cast it all on Me.

TO DO: PRAY: Lord Jesus, my empathetic High Priest, I lay before You the issues that burden me, including things I cannot share with anyone. By Your sacrifice for me, I access all the help and provision I require to fix these issues. Thank You for always being there for me. Amen.

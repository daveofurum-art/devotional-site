Not For Your Sin

Pastor David Ofurum

Psalms 34:19 KJV Many are the afflictions of the righteous: but the LORD delivereth him out of them all.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Everyone has troubles from time to time, including the righteous. Troubles are not always the consequences of sin, and neither do they mean that I have abandoned you. Rest assured, My child, that I am making a way out for you from your present troubles and your deliverance is near.

TO DO: DECLARE: I am the righteousness of God in Christ Jesus: I won’t yield to Satan’s mind games and guilty tags. My troubles are not God’s punishment for wrongs I’ve done. I know my Redeemer lives, and He is bringing me out victoriously. Blessed be His holy name.

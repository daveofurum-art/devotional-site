#GodsOracleToday #GOT Wednesday, 01Apr2026 USE YOUR DIVINE NATURE Pastor David Ofurum 2Peter 1:4 KJV Whereby are given unto us exceeding great and precious promises: that by these ye might be partakers of the divine nature, having escaped the corruption that is in the world through lust. Thus says the Lord: You have more than just promises in Me. By being in Christ, you become part of Me, possessing some of My divine attributes, like speaking things into existence. Welcome to April. Now, speak into existence what you want your April to look like. To do: DECREE: This is my April! In this month, I am fruitful. All things are working for my good. The lines are falling for me in pleasant places, and I have a goodly heritage. Good comes to me, and evil is far from me. In April, I excel, I win, I advance. Hallelujah!

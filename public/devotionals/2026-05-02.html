Things Will Make Sense

Pastor David Ofurum

Isaiah 40:4 KJV Every valley shall be exalted, and every mountain and hill shall be made low: and the crooked shall be made straight, and the rough places plain:

THUS SAYS THE LORD: You may have been putting in your best effort and yet, it seems like things are not adding up for you. Not to worry; I am making all things beautiful for you in its time. Things will begin to take shape for you. Things will begin to make sense. All things are aligning for your good.

TO DO: DECLARE: Father, I believe your word. I know that You are working behind the scenes for me. I prophesy to every valley in my life, be filled; to every mountain, be leveled; to every crooked path, be straightened; and to every rough place, be smooth in Jesus’ name.

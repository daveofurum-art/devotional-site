Your Growth Path

Pastor David Ofurum

James 1:3 KJV Knowing this, that the trying of your faith worketh patience.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Patience is not an easy-to-grow fruit, yet it is a must-have for full maturity. Patience never grows wild; it must be intentionally cultivated in the soil of your heart, with trials as fertilizer. You’re not being tried because I love you less but because you’re experiencing growth.

TO DO: PRAY: O Lord, help me to endure the difficulty of today’s trials so that I can enjoy the blessings of tomorrow’s maturity. Strengthen my faith with patience, Lord, so that I can handle greater opportunities. Thank You Your sufficient grace in Jesus’ name. Amen.

New Door, New Fight

Pastor David Ofurum

1Corinthians 16:9 KJV For a great door and effectual is opened unto me, and there are many adversaries.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Although I have opened a door for you that no one can shut, it doesn’t mean there won’t be people who will want to make it difficult for you to use the door. An open door often means the start of another fight. But fear not, I who opened the door am leading the fight too.

TO DO: DECLARE: I access every door God has opened for me. I am not afraid of the fight because every adversary must come to their knees as the hosts of Heaven fight for me. Every obstacle is cleared, and every mountain leveled for me in Jesus’ mighty name. Amen.

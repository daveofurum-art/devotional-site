Stay Upright

Pastor David Ofurum

Psalms 37:37 KJV Mark the perfect man, and behold the upright: for the end of that man is peace.

Yes, integrity still pays – it always does. It may seem like crooks are carrying the day and living large, but their perceived victory is only transient. The upright may seem to be losing out for a while, but My eyes are on them to ensure they have the last and longest laugh. So, maintain your integrity.

CONFESS: I have purposed in my heart to live right, and the Holy Spirit in me enables me to keep to it. I am not swayed by the prosperity of the wicked; it’s only for a while. With integrity, I not only win, but I build a lasting legacy to the glory of God. Hallelujah!

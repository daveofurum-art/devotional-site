Undignified Worship

Pastor David Ofurum

2Samuel 6:21 KJV And David said unto Michal, It was before the LORD, which chose me before thy father… to appoint me ruler… over Israel: therefore will I play before the LORD.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: It’s worship time, and you want to act dignified before your Maker? Whatever class or status you think you have, I gave it to you. There have been people from whom I stripped these things because of their pride. Don’t become one of them.

TO DO: DECLARE: O my spirit, worship your Maker. Whatever things are "gain" to you are dung in the presence of your God, the Creator of the universe. Lay down your crowns before the One who crowned you and worship Him with absolute humility and trembling.

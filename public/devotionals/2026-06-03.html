One View, One Access

Pastor David Ofurum

Genesis 6:16 KJV A window shalt thou make to the ark, and in a cubit shalt thou finish it above; and the door of the ark shalt thou set in the side thereof; with lower, second, and third stories shalt thou make it.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: As Noah’s ark had only one window and one door, so also there is only one view and one access to My salvation – Jesus Christ, My Son. He is the only way, the only truth, and the only life there is. No one comes to Me except through Him, neither is there salvation in any other but in Him.

TO DO: CONFESS: I come through the door of Jesus Christ. I place my faith in no other but Him. I believe in His ultimate sacrifice for my salvation. I receive His mercy and free gift of salvation and eternal life. I am now a child of God, and my name is written in the Book of Life. Thank You, Jesus, forever.

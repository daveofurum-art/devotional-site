Yours Won't Work

Pastor David Ofurum

Philippians 3:9 KJV And be found in him, not having mine own righteousness, which is of the law, but that which is through the faith of Christ, the righteousness which is of God by faith:

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Man? Be righteous without Christ? Impossible. Your own attempt at “righteousness” by your own works and morality is futile. It might impress men, but not Me. Righteousness is a gift you can only receive from Me through faith in My Son, Jesus Christ. Everything else stinks.

TO DO: DECLARE: My faith is in Jesus, my righteousness. My good works are products of the gift of righteousness that I’ve received from Him, not an attempt to achieve righteousness. Thank you, Jesus, for this precious gift.

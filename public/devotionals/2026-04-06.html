Undisputable Truth

Pastor David Ofurum

John 21:13-14 KJV 13 Jesus then cometh, and taketh bread, and giveth them, and fish likewise. 14 This is now the third time that Jesus shewed himself to his disciples, after that he was risen from the dead.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Some attempt to dispute My resurrection. But in the mouth of two or three witnesses, every truth is established. After I arose, I made multiple appearances, performing living human activities to show beyond doubt that it is I. Children, your faith is not in cunningly devised fables but in eternal truth.

TO DO: DECLARE: Jesus Christ, the Son of God, crucified for my transgressions, buried for my redemption, and resurrected on the third day for my justification, You alone are God. I will tell the whole world with certainty that You rose again and You live forever more. Hallelujah!

Philippians 2:10 KJV That at the name of Jesus every knee should bow, of things in heaven, and things in earth, and things under the earth;

TO DO: DECREE: Every giant standing against me comes to it knees now in the name of Jesus. Every authority yields to my demands in the name of Jesus. Every unpleasant diagnosis is canceled in the name of Jesus. Jesus is glorified in my life today and always. Amen.

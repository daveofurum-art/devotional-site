I am Your Pavilion

Pastor David Ofurum

Genesis 1:9 KJV And God said, Let the waters under the heaven be gathered together unto one place, and let the dry land appear: and it was so.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Let the trouble waters of disease, depression, oppression, failures, inadequacies, and the past be gathered together and be calm forever in your life. Let the dry land of healing, peace, strength, help, deliverance, success, and victory now appear for you.

TO DO: DECLARE: Amen! It is done in my life as the Lord has said. My peace shall now appear, and every help that has been hidden from me shall now manifest. I have rest and respite from every flood that has troubled my life. I am free, safe, and strong. I have stability in Jesus’ name.

Right and Guided

Pastor David Ofurum

Judges 20:18 KJV And the children of Israel arose, and went up to the house of God, and asked counsel of God, and said, Which of us shall go up first to the battle against the children of Benjamin? And the LORD said, Judah shall go up first.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: It was a civil war to purge the nation of abominable evil, yet they sought counsel from Me. Blessed is that nation that orders its ways in My counsel rather than in presumption. Even when you know you’re doing right, seek My guidance. Otherwise, you could be right and yet lose.

TO DO: DECLARE: Lord, I trust in Your strategic guidance with all my heart. I will not lean on my own understanding, even when I’m sure I’m on the right track. In all my ways, I will acknowledge You, and You shall direct my path. By Your wisdom alone, I fight and triumph always.

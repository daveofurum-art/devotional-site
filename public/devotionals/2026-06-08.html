My Image in You

Pastor David Ofurum

Colossians 3:10 KJV and have put on the new man, which is renewed in knowledge after the image of him that created him:

THUS SAYS THE LORD: To have My image is to know Me. When I made man in My image, it was the knowledge of Me that I gave Him, making him different from other creatures and allowing him to enjoy dominion, until he chose to be apart from Me and fell. In Christ, My image in you is renewed by your knowledge of Me. The more you know Me, the more like Me you become.

TO DO: PRAY: Holy Spirit, open the eyes of my understanding to know the Father more each day. As I look into the mirror of Your word, the perfect law of liberty, and I draw knowledge of You, change me into the same image of You that I see in Your word, from glory to glory, in Jesus’ name. Amen.

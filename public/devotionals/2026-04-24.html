My Hand Is On You

Pastor David Ofurum

Ezekiel 1:3 KJV The word of the LORD came expressly unto Ezekiel the priest, the son of Buzi, in the land of the Chaldeans by the river Chebar; and the hand of the LORD was there upon him.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Even in the land of captivity, My hand was upon Ezekiel. Just because you’re going through tough times doesn’t mean I have abandoned you. Yeah, though you walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I am with you to protect, strengthen, deliver, and use you.

TO DO: CONFESS: My Father is with me. He will never leave or forsake me. His hand is mightily upon me despite any contrary suggestion by my circumstances. I feel His love and warm embrace. Therefore, I shall not be moved. I have peace in the midst of the storm because of the hand of my God upon me.

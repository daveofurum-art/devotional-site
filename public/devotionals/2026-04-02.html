Let Them Go

Pastor David Ofurum

Judges 7:7 KJV And the LORD said unto Gideon, By the three hundred men that lapped will I save you, and deliver the Midianites into thine hand: and let all the other people go every man unto his place.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: You really do not need everyone for you to succeed. You just need to let some people go because overcrowding can kill a good vision. But you need to trust Me for guidance in knowing the right people to let go because losing the wrong people can also set you back.

TO DO: PRAY: Father, I trust in You with all my heart, and I will not lean on my own understanding. Show me the people whose continued presence in my life could harm Your purpose for me, and give me the courage to let then go in Jesus’ name. Amen.

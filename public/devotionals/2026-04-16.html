Don't Serve Sin

Pastor David Ofurum

Romans 6:6 KJV Knowing this, that our old man is crucified with him, that the body of sin might be destroyed, that henceforth we should not serve sin.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: Sinful living is slavery, not freedom. I killed your sinful nature on the cross so that if you’re truly surrendered to Me, you can live free from the control of sin. Any doctrine that implies My grace is a license to live comfortably in sin is a trick to trap you back into slavery. Beware.

TO DO: CONFESS: I am crucified with Christ. Nevertheless, I live; yet not I, but Christ lives in me. My sinful man is dead in Christ. The life I now live, I live by faith in the grace of Christ that keeps me free from sin. I’m no longer a slave to sin; I am a child of God. Hallelujah!

Stop Helping Hell

Pastor David Ofurum

2 Corinthians 5:11 KJV Knowing therefore the terror of the Lord, we persuade men; but we are made manifest unto God; and I trust also are made manifest in your consciences.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: You’re not seriously evangelizing because you either are not aware of the gravity of punishment that awaits sinners in the afterlife or you no longer care. Either way, you’re passively helping to grow the number of people going to hell. Stop doing that. Start telling the world about the consequences of sin and the escape that’s only found in Christ.

TO DO: PRAY: Lord Jesus, take away from me the selfishness that is content with being saved and Heaven-bound and nonchalant about the terrible fate that awaits people who do not know You. Let genuine concern for their eternal safety stir me daily to preach Christ to the unsaved, in Your name I pray. Amen.

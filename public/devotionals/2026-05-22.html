Smiling At You

Pastor David Ofurum

Numbers 6:25 EASY The Lord will smile at you and he will be very kind to you.

THUS SAYS THE LORD: You are My beloved child in whom I am well pleased. I am smiling at you, that’s why your way will never be dark and slippery, and your enemies will never triumph over you. My kindness will bring you ease in your pursuits, and you will go from one testimony to another.

TO DO: CONFESS: I am blessed by the Lord. I shine in all things because His face shines on me. He causes the lines to fall for me in pleasant places. My household and I enjoy God’s favour everywhere we go. God’s goodness is evident in all that we do; we always win. Glory to God!

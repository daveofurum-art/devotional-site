Walking in Faith
“For we walk by faith, not by sight.” – 2 Corinthians 5:7
Faith is not always about seeing the full path ahead, but trusting God step by step. Even when things seem uncertain, God is working behind the scenes for your good.

Today, choose to trust Him beyond what your eyes can see. Let your faith be stronger than your fears.
